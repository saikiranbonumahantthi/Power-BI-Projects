> **Revenue intelligence dashboard for AtliQ Grands hotel chain — tracking RevPAR, ADR, occupancy, and platform performance across properties.**

## 📌 Overview
AtliQ Grands is a luxury hotel chain losing market share. This Power BI dashboard gives the revenue team real-time visibility into pricing and occupancy strategy.

## 💡 Key Metrics
| KPI | Insight |
|---|---|
| RevPAR | Weekend 15% higher than weekday |
| ADR | Presidential rooms command highest rate |
| Occupancy % | Mumbai properties outperform Bangalore |
| Realisation % | OTAs show lower realisation than direct bookings |
